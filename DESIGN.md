# TRAZOT Design Language

## Mood: Professional, Trustworthy, Vibrant
- **Primary:** #FF6B35 (Orange - Energy, Action)
- **Secondary:** #1A2B4C (Navy - Trust, Stability)
- **Success:** #10B981 (Green - Growth, Verification)
- **Background:** #F8FAFC (Light Gray/Blue tint)

## Typography:
- **Sans:** Inter (Clean, readable)
- **Display:** Outfit (Modern, friendly)

## Interaction:
- Subtle scale-ups on hover for cards.
- Fade-in animations for page transitions.
- High-contrast buttons for calls to action.
